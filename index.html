Stay&Play

Our Stay&Play rooms offer a safe environment for children under 10 to explore, learn and play at a pace that is right for them.

Playing is a crucial part of every child's early development.

Here at Play Grounds, we offer open-ended opportunities for your child to think independently about play and develop life-lasting skills such as creativity, problem solving and resilience.

Event room

With an event room on site, there's opportunity for everyone! Plan a party, host a workshop, or join us for our monthly pop-up art classes. Stay in the loop by signing up to our email list!

Friday Night Sitter

On select Friday's from 5-7pm, drop the kids off and enjoy an evening to yourself. Children ages 5-12 years can have fun with qualified staff while doing arts and crafts, playing or whatever else is included with their ticket. These nights are never "drop-in" and must be booked ahead of time. See above for current events being offered.
