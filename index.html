Event room

With an event room on site, there's opportunity for everyone! Plan a party, host an event or join us for our monthly pop-up art classes. Stay in the loop by signing up to our email list!

Drop&Shop

Come and check out The Common Ground Warehouse for a stress-free shopping or dining experience while your potty-trained kids have a blast in our supervised playroom. Our qualified staff will ensure that your children are safe and entertained while you take care of your errands. Plus, you can even use our workspace to get some work done or relax while your kids play. It's a win-win situation for everyone!

Stay&Play

Play Grounds Stay&Play room is a safe environment for children under 4 to explore, learn and play at a pace that is right for them.

Playing is a crucial part of every child's early development.

Here at Play Grounds, we offer open-ended opportunities for your child to think independently about play and develop life-lasting skills such as creativity, problem solving and resilience.
