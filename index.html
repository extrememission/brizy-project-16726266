Event room

With an event room on site, there's opportunity for everyone! Plan a party, host a workshop, or join us for our monthly pop-up art classes. Stay in the loop by signing up to our email list!

Gift Shop

Children of all ages can play for free in our gift shop equipped with a jungle gym, slide, tree house, and tire swing! Browse our sensory bins, while the kids burn off energy. This room is included when you book a private party through Play Grounds!

Stay&Play

Our Stay&Play room is a safe environment for children under 4 to explore, learn and play at a pace that is right for them.

Playing is a crucial part of every child's early development.

Here at Play Grounds, we offer open-ended opportunities for your child to think independently about play and develop life-lasting skills such as creativity, problem solving and resilience.
